About us:

We are a company that is dedicated to the general remodeling of houses. doing home improvements. We are dedicated to painting, remodeling of kitchens, bathrooms, installation of all kinds of ceramics, we do Deck, wooden floors and much more.

We focus on you, quality and keeping you in the know. You can count to us to deliver a positive remodeling experience and a repeatable process allow us to reliably complete project on time at the promised quality level.

Maximize results by combining performance, efficiency and quality in each project, satisfying each of the requests and requirements of our customers. using the best materials, tools and trained personnel for each task that will lead us to meet the estimated time in the projects with their specific qualities and characteristics.

History of the company:

Our beginnings date back to 2010 in the state of Connecticut, New Haven, where our team came together to offer basic services to nearby homes, Since then we have never stopped in the effort and sacrifice to bring the best services in home improvement, developing techniques and skills that have allowed us to become a team that is characterized by giving the best in each of the projects we have done, receiving congratulations and thanks from our customers.
